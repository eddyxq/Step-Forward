The team

President

Cole Thiessen

Cole is a 3rd Year Business/Computer Science concentrator. He recognizes the gap between interpersonal and technical skills in almost every industry and decided to bridge this gap by pursuing a combined degree. He has began to focus on philanthropy and educating others in his 3rd year. He concieved CPUS with the goal of developing his own technical skills while teaching other students basic programming and helping local businesses.

VP Op-Fi

Jose Menjivar

Jose is a 3rd Year Finance/ Computer Science concentrator. He recognizes the importance of technical skills in almost every aspect of business, whether it be building business software or solving complex business problems. He has worked in business development, management, compliance and supervision. In his 3rd year, he shifted his focus to volunteering and helping others and helped with the conception of CPUS.

VP Marketing

Tasia Ntwari

Tasia is a 3rd year Supply Chain concentrator. She knows that technology is rapidly changing the business environment and would like to enrich her knowledge on these advancements by learning technical skills.

VP Events

Jason Lyster

Jason is 3rd Year Finance/ Computer Science concentrator. He recognizes that business knowledge is essential for success in any industry, which inspired him to pursue a dual major. Jason has worked in a number of projects including full-stack development, database maintenance, and web-design. He is responsible for leading groups of new software developers and ensuring that every project CPUS creates is well designed and functional.
